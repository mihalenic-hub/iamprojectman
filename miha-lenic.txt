## What I do

I bridge the Sales-Delivery gap for Trade and Customs Technology companies operating in B2G (government) and B2B (corporate) segments. I implement a structured 4-step engagement methodology called the Bridge Blueprint.

The Bridge Blueprint covers:

1. Deal Filter — qualify and filter bad-fit projects before contract negotiations begin
1. Customer Decoder — structured discovery to uncover what customers actually need
1. Offer Generator — proposals that Sales, Delivery, and Legal can all commit to
1. Hassle-Free Handover — complete delivery kickoff package so execution starts on day one

After the engagement, I either exit (handing off the full package) or stay embedded as a Delivery Advisor or Sales Bridge Advisor on a retainer or milestone basis.
